Caltech Squirrels

About the Caltech Squirrels

Caltech is home to many squirrels. Just taking a walk around campus, you will see them running up and down trees, sitting on the ground, and eating nuts (sometimes you will catch them with something bigger, such as a pear or sandwich bread). These squirrels are known as Fox Squirrels. Interestingly, they are actually invasive to California, but they're cute to look at! The best squirrel spotting places on campus are near the Keck Center and the trees around Beckman Lawn. However, you can find squirrels in many other places on Campus.
