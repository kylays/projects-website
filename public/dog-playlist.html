Dog Playlist

Generate a New Dog Playlist:

About

This project combines peoples' love for music and dogs! The page generates a playlist where each song is based off of a letter in a selected dog breed or random dog breed chosen from the Dog API. This API has many pictures for each dog breed, so one is chosen randomly within the breed. The songs are found using the Spotify API. The songs are chosen randomly from the list of the current top 500 most popular songs in the selected genre. Many songs have a playable snippet, but if the play and pause buttons are missing, the Spotify API is missing a preview clip of the song.

The wireframe below is an initial concept design for the 'Dog Playlist.' This design was altered in specific ways to increase usability and make the page more interactive.

A major change is the upgrade of then "new dog playlist" button to the dog breed and genre menus and the "Go!"" button. This change allows for a more interactive and customizable experience. Please also note the placement of the buttons. They are placed at the bottom of the playlist once a playlist is generated to follow along the user experience of scrolling down the page to look at the playlist song by song. At the end of this process, people will likely be ready to make a new playlist and can easily access the buttons.

Another change to the initial wireframe is including pause buttons. Initially, I only included play buttons in the design, but once working on the webpage, I realized that pause buttons were necessary so people could listen to multiple snippets without the music overlapping.
