Kendall-Frost Mural: Design Process

About

The Kendall-Frost Nature Reserve is a large coastal marsh managed by UC San Diego. It is home to lots of wildlife and scientifc experiements. For a long time, a bungalow was located near the marsh for research and adminstrative purposes. To beautify the bungalow and increase awareness about wetlands conservation, I worked with Director Isabelle Kay to design the following mural.
